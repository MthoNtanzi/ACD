About us

Ashdown Community Development is committed to upholding the Constitution of South Africa and all applicable legislation. The organisation promotes human dignity, equality, non- discrimination, and access to services for all individuals, including marginalized and vulnerable populations such as MSM, transgender persons, sex workers, ex-offenders, people who use drugs, women, men, and children.

Services

Our services include:

  • Trauma counselling and emotional support
  • Crisis intervention
  • GBV (Gender-Based Violence) response services
  • Referral to healthcare, legal, and social services
  • Survivor support programmes

Focus Areas

  • Gender-based violence (GBV)
  • Sexual abuse and assault
  • Emotional and psychological trauma
